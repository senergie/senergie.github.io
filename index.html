Right now, 1.3 billion people are living in energy poverty.

In our modern world, access to electricity is crucial to human well-being and economic development.
In Senegal alone, seven million people lack modern energy services.
With this vision, five Belgian students founded Sénérgie, to bring light to rural Senegal.

No Electricity?

The electrification rate in rural Senegal is less than 25%.

Kerosene?

Families are relying on kerosene or other substitutes for lighting: dirty, inefficient and expensive.

Solar Energy!

Sénérgie offers clean and renewable energy at an affordable price.

We believe in offering the full experience of modern energy

Sénérgie distributes Pico Solar Home Systems, created by Fosera. Our world-class solar kits include several high-quality LED lights, a radio, and multiple phone chargers. A close cooperation with a micro-finance institute enables Sénérgie to reach low income customers, helping them to overcome the upfront costs by spreading the payments on a monthly basis.
