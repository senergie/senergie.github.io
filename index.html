Right now, 1.3 billion people live in energy poverty.

In our modern world, access to electricity is crucial to human well-being and economic development. In Senegal alone, seven million people lack modern energy services. With this vision, five Belgian students founded Sénérgie, to bring light to rural Senegal and help achieve the Sustainable Development Goals!

No Electricity?

The electrification rate in rural Senegal is less than 25%.

Kerosene?

Families rely on kerosene or other substitutes for lighting: dirty, inefficient and expensive.

Solar Energy!

Sénérgie offers clean and renewable energy at an affordable price.

We believe in offering the full experience of modern energy

Sénérgie distributes ‘Pico Solar Home Systems’, created by Fosera. Our world-class solar kit includes four high-quality LED lights, a radio, and multiple phone chargers. In order to reach even the lowest incomes of the rural population, we have included ‘Pay-As-You-Go’ technology into the solar system. This allows people to overcome the upfront cost and spread the payments on a monthly basis. Afterwards, we give our customers the chance to upgrade their systems to a larger solar system, including appliances like fans, TV’s, …
